🌌 Amazing Space Facts

Expand the questions below to discover fascinating facts about our Solar System and the universe.

Earth is called the Blue Planet because over 70% of its surface is covered by water, making it appear blue from space.

Mars appears red because its surface contains iron oxide, commonly known as rust.

Saturn's rings are made of billions of pieces of ice, rock, and dust orbiting the planet.

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System and is more than 11 times wider than Earth.

Space is mostly empty, so light doesn't scatter like it does in Earth's atmosphere. That's why it appears dark.
